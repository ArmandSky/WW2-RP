US Marines Storm Valencia: A New Chapter in the Spanish Civil War

Valencia, Spain – In a stunning turn of events that has reshaped the course of the Spanish Civil War, United States Marines landed on the shores of Valencia today, seizing the strategic port city. The swift and decisive operation, codenamed "Operation Levante," marks a significant escalation of US involvement in the conflict.

The invasion force, comprised of the 45th Marine Regiment, encountered little resistance as they stormed the city. The Republican government, already reeling from heavy losses on the front lines, was unable to mount a significant defense. The rapid advance of Nationalist forces, backed by German and Italian troops, had left Valencia vulnerable to attack.

The US intervention is a major blow to the Republican cause and a significant victory for the Nationalist forces led by General Francisco Franco. With the capture of Valencia, the Nationalists have gained a crucial strategic foothold on the Mediterranean coast, allowing them to resupply their troops and launch further offensives.

Finnish Volunteers Decimate Republican Forces in Bilbao

Bilbao, Spain – In a stunning display of military prowess, the Finnish Volunteer Army, fighting alongside the Nationalist forces, has inflicted a crushing defeat on the Republican 2nd Army in the strategic city of Bilbao. The Finnish volunteers, renowned for their winter warfare expertise, brought a level of tactical sophistication and firepower that overwhelmed the Republican defenders. Utilizing their superior tank and artillery assets, they launched a series of coordinated attacks that breached the Republican defenses and encircled the city.

The Republican forces, already weakened by lack of international aid, were unable to withstand the onslaught. The Finnish volunteers, known for their tenacity and discipline, fought with ferocity, pushing the Republicans back and inflicting heavy casualties.

The swift and decisive victory in Bilbao marks a significant turning point in the Spanish Civil War. It demonstrates the impact that even a relatively small force of highly trained and well-equipped soldiers can have on the battlefield. The Finnish volunteers' success has earned them the admiration of the Nationalist forces and has solidified their reputation as one of the most effective fighting forces in the conflict.

The future of the Spanish Civil War now hangs in the balance. With the fall of Bilbao, the Republicans face a daunting challenge to maintain their hold on the remaining territory. The Nationalist forces, bolstered by the Finnish volunteers, are poised to launch further offensives and bring the war to a decisive conclusion.

Madrid Falls: A Brutal End to the Republican Hold

Madrid, Spain - In a relentless assault that lasted for weeks, the Nationalist forces, bolstered by German reinforcements, have finally breached the defenses of Madrid. The once-impregnable city, a symbol of Republican resistance, has fallen to the combined might of the Nationalist and German forces.

The 1st and 2nd Nationalist Armies, aided by the German 1st Motorized and 3rd Panzer Divisions, launched a coordinated attack on the city, after the 1st & 2nd Panzer Divisions have broken through the flanks protecting the capital, exploiting the weaknesses in the Republican defenses. The German forces, with their superior firepower and tactical expertise, proved to be a decisive factor in the battle.

The Republican 1st Army, though well-trained and equipped, was overwhelmed by the sheer force of the Nationalist and German assault. Fierce street-to-street fighting erupted as the Republican defenders fought desperately to hold their ground. However, the relentless bombardment from the air and the relentless advance of ground forces proved too much for the beleaguered defenders.

The fall of Madrid marks a significant turning point in the Spanish Civil War. With the capital city in Nationalist hands, the Republican cause has been dealt a devastating blow. The remaining Republican forces are now scattered and isolated, facing the prospect of total defeat.

Uncofirmed reports suggest the Remaining Republican Leadership managed to escape the attack on Madrid, and have regrouped in Granada, however without international aid, morale & public sentiment is faltering for Republicans and many speculate it is only a matter of time before surrender is inevitable.

Finland: Mannerheim Dissolves Parliament!

Shocking news for Finland this morning, as the Field Marshal dissolved parliament, and several SDP and National Progressive Party politicians have been arrested on charges of collaboration with 'Anti-Finnish factions'. In a bold move, Mannerheim led an honor guard of 100 veterans from the wars with Norway and Sweden into the parliament building, declaring the Republic of Finland ended effective immediately. The constitution of 1919, establishing the Republic, has also been declared null. Mannerheim, as the politicians were led away, spoke to the people of Helsinki:

"My fellow Finns. Today marks the momentous occasion where we throw off the shackles of weak, vacillating men who threaten to leave our nation in ruins or in the clutches of foreign powers. The weak-minded, who shield themselves with false ideals and promises, have led us to nothing but decay, and have stood in the way of what needs to be done for all Finns time and time again. When I signed the constitution, I did so with reluctance in my heart. When I served as a regent, I had hope for a strong government to guide our people now freed from Tsarist oppression. Instead, we have seen the snakes of the civil war return under new names. Now calling themselves, liberals, socialists, progressives, and other false names to hide their treason. I have led the nation, under attack by the equally misguided governments of Norway and Sweden, to victory. I have led us to unprecedented prosperity. Where did the factories that now employ you come from? Where did the rich wealth from commerce arise? Where did the glory that holds Finland up as a world power come from? I tell you; it has come from the military. From the men who serve with honor, who stood the protect the Finnish people, and now the Norwegian and Swedish people, from the evils of weak governance and decadence. And I led them!”

“And so, we now understand the actions I have taken today. I have taken the actions necessary to seize power from the weak men who threaten the new nation we have forged through conquest and unification. All the peoples of our nation, from those born with the rights awarded to them as Finns, to those now granted those same rights as new citizens, deserve strong leadership, from men of experience in matters of war and peace. As such, I hereby formally dissolve the weak Republic under which we were forced to live following the end of my regency, and instead declare myself King of Greater Finland! Our people stand united against all who would threaten our prosperity and sovereignty. Never again shall we be forced to bow. Never again shall we allow others to dictate to us. Never again shall the North be disunited! God bless Finland and her people!”

The crowd was reportedly enthusiastic, cheering for the famous Field Marshall who has become so beloved as a military leader. Many readers will note the widespread publication of his military exploits in Finnish newspapers throughout the past two years. The crowd was carefully watched by the local garrison. The corps protecting the capital has reportedly wholeheartedly endorsed the new king, officially becoming the Royal Guard Corps. It seems that any opposition is not forthcoming, especially as the most likely candidates for dissent are led away in cuffs and nationalist and monarchist politicians are promoted to positions of power. The future of Finland seems to be set on a new path, with new leadership guiding it into the tumultuous future.

Arms Expo Munich 1936

As conflict is ensuing in several parts of the world, The Arms expo comes together again, this time in Munich Germany. Where nations big and small can showcase their latest Military Equipment and sell to prospective interested nations. Alongside the expo a Military show where some nations are invited to bring their newest equipment to demonstrate live, what each nations industries have been up to.

Germany stole the spotlight so far showcasing their latest variant of the Panzer IV B variant as their new mainstay Medium Tank Alongside the Panzer 3D. It is not only on the ground where their equipment shown, they also presented for the first time to the public the Junkers 87 "Stuka" Dive Bomber, a revolutionary design in the category of light bomber doctrine. All of which the German Representatives claim are currently showing their quality in the Spanish civil war and have been actively taking part, showing these vehicles aren't just for the displays at expo's but are effective fighting machines.

On the commercial front they also displayed the Junkers Ju 86 Passenger Airliner, showcasing an impressive range and seating capacity for a commercial airliner.

As the Expo is on the whole month of November we look forward to seeing what else is on showcase at the Expo.
